Frequently Asked Questions



On this page I will try to answer as many frequently asked questions as possible, if I have missed anything out then please feel free to contact me via my contact page and I will update my site to include any questions I may have missed out.



How do I change the background colour?

You can change the background colour by click the gear icon on the right hand side of the navigation bar located at the top of the screen. Doing so will open up a drop down bar below the navigation bar, you can then choose one of the background colour options under the ‘background colour’ heading.



How can I change the font size?


You can change the background colour by click the gear icon on the right hand side of the navigation bar located at the top of the screen. Doing so will open up a drop down bar below the navigation bar, you can then change the font size by clicking one of the options under the ‘font size’ heading.



How can I change the language?

You can change the language by clicking the ‘select language’ button icon on the right hand side of the navigation bar located at the top of the screen. Doing so will open a drop down bar with a number of languages to choose from, click your desired language and then the page will automatically change to the chosen language.
