The Matrix Project!

For the last ten years programmers around the world and I have been working on an ambitious project to fully immerse users within a digital reality. In this digital reality, it anything would be possible and the user’s ability to do what they want will only be limited by their own imaginations!


It would be down to you to decide what type of life you lead within the matrix, whether that be a regular life, i.e Getting a job, playing sports, socialising with friends, going to the cinema, playing video games or just going for a walk in the park!

Or if that life isn’t for you, we’ve designed a number or downloadable packages that the user will be able ‘advance‘ themselves to live a life more suiting of their own lifestyle. The downloadable packages come in a great deal of variety and we can assure you that there will be something for everybody (Including grandma!). These packages can range from beginner to advance in ability and then we have packages in pretty much anything you can think of! From knitting to computer hacking, mixed martial arts to piloting helicopters, firearms use to being good at Sudoku, the sky really is the limit! (Well not really, some users even have the ability to fly, albeit this is only recommended fort the most advanced users!)


Security


Although this is project is all good and fun for most users, we had to have a way or regulating users experience. We didn’t want some users simply running round, shooting people and spoiling the experience for others! So to regulate this, we had to design our own in-house security bots known as ‘agents’. These bots would be extremely powerful within the VR environment and no user would be to overpower them. Agents also give us the ability to pinpoint any users in the real world who may be ‘cheating’, any users found cheating would be dealt with very ‘firmly’.



Bugs


Although the project was very successful it was still not without bugs, some users have been reported to have been approached by in game bots offering them blue and red pills. We can categorically say we did not code these bots into the Matrix! Anybody who encounters anything like this should report this immediately to our admin team.
